BENIFITS OF DANCING

1.Physical Benefits
✅ Improves flexibility, coordination, and balance.
✅ Strengthens muscles and enhances posture.
✅ Boosts cardiovascular health and stamina.
2. Mental & Emotional Benefits
✅ Reduces stress and anxiety by releasing endorphins.
✅ Enhances memory and cognitive skills.
✅ Builds self-confidence and body positivity.
3. Social & Cultural Benefits
✅ Encourages teamwork and communication in group dances.
✅ Helps preserve cultural heritage and traditions.
✅ Brings people together through festivals, performances, and competitions.
